Summary

During her family's move to the suburbs, a sullen 10-year-old girl named Chihiro wanders into a world ruled by gods, witches, and spirits, and where humans are changed into beasts. When her parents are magically transformed into pigs, Chihiro must take a job working at a bathhouse for spirits to find a way to free herself and her parents and return to the human world. Along the way, she encounters mysterious allies and dangerous adversaries, learning valuable lessons about courage, identity, and environmentalism.
